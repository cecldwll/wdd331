Theme

After reviewing the examples, we have settled on Glitch as our theme!
The idea is to incorporate bright vibrant colors, with animations to
make our page look “broken”, but in an intentional and refined way.
We plan to accomplish this with various methods like gradients,
borders, animations, etc. Most of our effects will incorporate
backgrounds, borders, and scroll animations so they will be visible in
mobile, but we will also have multiple hover effects for desktop users.
